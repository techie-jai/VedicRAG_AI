This is part III of the Sacred Books of the East
Satapatha Brahamana translation, containing Books V, VI and VII.
This portion is notable for the description of the
extended Vedic creation myth.
This includes a mention of a 'cosmic egg,' a key element of other
widely dispersed creation myths.
