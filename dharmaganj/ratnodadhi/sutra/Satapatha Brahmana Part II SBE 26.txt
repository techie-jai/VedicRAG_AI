This is part II of the Sacred Books of the East
Satapatha Brahamana translation, containing Books III and IV.
This volume is concerned with the construction of the ceremonial space
used in the Soma sacrifice.
Every act, word and item used in the ritual has deep symbolic meaning,
and numerous connections to vedic mythology and deities.
